===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses speaks a poetic song to the people of Israel. Moses continues to use parallelism to emphasize what he is saying. (See: [[:my:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **Were it not that I feared the provocation of the enemy** - "If it were not for their enemies' taunts"
  * **judge mistakenly** - "misunderstand"
  * **Our hand is exalted** - AT: "Our strength has triumphed" or "Our power has won" (See: [[:my:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
