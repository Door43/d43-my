===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **on stringed instruments.**  - This can also be written as a sentence. AT: “It is to be accompanied by people playing stringed instruments” or “People should play stringed instruments with this song.”
  * **A maschil of David**  - See how you translated this in [[:my:bible:notes:psa:032:001|32:1-2]]
  * **Ziphites**  - people from the city of Ziph in the Judean mountains, southeast of Hebron. (See: [[:my:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Does David not hide himself with us?**  - AT: "David is hiding himself with us." (See: [[:my:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **by your name**  - AT: "by your power" (See: [[:my:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **judge me in your might**  - AT: "decide if I have done what is right or wrong"
  * **give ear**  - AT: "listen" (See: [[:my:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **strangers**  - "people I don't know"
  * **pitiless men**  - men who have no mercy
