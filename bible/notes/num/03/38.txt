===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **his sons** - Here the word "his" refers to Aaron.
  * **for the fulfillment of the duties** - AT: "to perform the duties" (See: [[:my:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **must be put to death** - AT: "you must kill" or "must die" (See: [[:my:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **twenty-two thousand men** - AT: "22,000 men" (See: [[my:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
