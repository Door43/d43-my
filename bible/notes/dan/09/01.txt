===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **In the first year of Darius…in the first year of his reign**  - "In year one of Darius…in year one of his reign"
  * **over the realm**  - "over the country" or "over the kingdom"
  * **reign**  - "rule" (See how you translated "reign" in [[:my:bible:notes:dan:02:01|Daniel 1:1]])
  * **Yahweh's word that had came to Jeremiah the prophet **- This is the message that God gave to Jeremiah. He had to preach this to the people.
  * **that the number of years would be seventy **- AT: "it would be seventy years"
