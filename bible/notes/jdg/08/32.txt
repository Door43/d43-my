===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a good old age**  - AT: "very old" (See: [[:my:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **Ophrah**  - See how you translated the name of this place in [[:my:bible:notes:jdg:06:11|6:11]].
  * **Abiezrites**  - See how you translated the name of this people group in [[:my:bible:notes:jdg:06:11|6:11]].
  * **turned again**  - AT: "turned away from God" (See:[[:my:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit|Explicit and Implicit]])
  * **prostituted themselves by worshiping the Baals**  - AT: gave themselves to worship the images of the god Baal, like adultresses leave their husbands and go to other men. . (See: [[:my:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **Baal Berith**  - This is the name of a false god. (See: [[:my:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
