===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **He said to them** - "Moses said to the people of Israel"
  * **Fix your mind on** - AT: “Pay attention to” or “Think about” (See: [[:my:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I have witnessed to you today** - "that I am giving today as a witness against you"
  * **your children** - "your children and descendants"
  * **this is no trivial matter for you** - "this is not a matter for you to ignore" or "the law is not just empty words." AT: "you must pay attention to what the law says" (See: [[:my:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **because it is your life** - "because you will live if you obey it"
