===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses speaks a poetic song to the people of Israel. Moses continues to use parallelism to emphasize what he is saying. (See: [[:my:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

  * **his sons and his daughters** - This refers to the people of Israel whom Yahweh gave life and made into a nation. (See: [[:my:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **I will hide my face from them**  - AT: "I will turn away from them" or "I will stop helping them" (See: [[:my:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **I will see what their end will be** - "I will see what becomes of them"
